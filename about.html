Breanna Robinson is a multi-disciplinary artist who lives and works in Chicago. She earned her BFA from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in 2017. Her work has been shown in Chicago at The Silver Room, Free Range Gallery, SAIC Sullivan Galleries,The Dojo Chicago ++, and at St. Agnes, Berlin.
