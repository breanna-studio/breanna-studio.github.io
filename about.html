Artist Statement

As an artist I identify as printmaker, coder, and collaborator with the processes and materials I work with. Prevalent themes in my work include nostalgia (and time, broadly), femininity, media and technology in the context of Black American culture, history, and traditions. My go-to mediums include printmaking, collage, drawing, and coding. Projects take shape through a mix of hand + digital renderings and image manipulation.

Bio

Breanna Robinson (she/her) is a multidisciplinary artist who lives and works in Chicago. She earned a BFA from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago (SAIC) in 2017, and has shown work at The International Print Center in New York and Fluffy Crimes, Free Range Gallery, WeSpace Gallery, and SAIC Sullivan Galleries in Chicago, among others.
