BLURCAM-CHROMA is an interactive webcam app that enables the viewer to meet their reflection at different levels of focus and hue while stream-of-consciousness style text appears, scrambles, and disappears. It was created to evoke a dream-like and moody sensation, mimicking fleeting thoughts and hazy recall.

text, css and javascript, raspberry pi, camera. 2022.
