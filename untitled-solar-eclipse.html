Untitled (solar eclipse)

In spiritual astrology, solar eclipses are viewed as periods of profound introspection and deep transformation, which I intended to capture a moment of in Untitled (Solar Eclipse). A Black woman sits still in an empty room. A partial solar eclipse in the night sky sets the background – but she has taken pause after catching her own reflection. It is a moment of vulnerability and rawness that we have all experienced in some way. We unravel, reviewing choices we’ve made, assessing our emotional wellness, and considering how much we really know ourselves.
