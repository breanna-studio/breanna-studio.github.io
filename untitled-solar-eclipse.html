Untitled (solar eclipse)

In spiritual astrology, a solar eclipse is often viewed as a period of profound introspection and deep transformation. One such moment is Untitled (Solar Eclipse). A Black woman sits still in an empty room. A partial solar eclipse in the night sky sets the background – but she has taken pause after catching her own reflection. It is a moment of vulnerability and rawness familiar to us all. We unravel, reviewing choices we’ve made, assessing our emotional wellness, and considering how much we really know ourselves.
