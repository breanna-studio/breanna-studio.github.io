The Play Spaces series draws on the iconically hazy, imperfect visual textures of risograph printing to evoke a sense of indistinct recollection and nostalgia. These works explore the relationship between memory and time, as historic images of parks from my childhood on the South Side of Chicago morph to suggest ghosts of a past that eludes personal memory.
